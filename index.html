The hard problem

Updates are not
all alike.

“Guidance rose to $36B” may replace an earlier estimate. A same-day contradictory report may need human review. A new metric may simply belong alongside the old one. LIANS treats those relationships differently.

It starts with structured metadata and broad candidate retrieval, applies deterministic financial rules where possible, then reserves LLM adjudication for ambiguous cases. Every decision carries confidence and a rationale that can be inspected later.
